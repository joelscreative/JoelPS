HELLO!

I'm a designer from the sunny state of Florida, USA. But now I live and work in Shinjuku, Tokyo. よろしくお願いします!

My combined love for technology and graphic design led me to UX design. I've had some great opportunities to work at NPOs, schools, and several small businesses internationally. I enjoy playing with interactive technology, and I'm constantly learning and applying new tricks to creatively solve problems.

I'm a designer at heart, so I design whenever I get a chance. Whether working on a print project for my friend's band, illustrating invitation cards for a relative's wedding, or coding a blog for an volunteer organization. I love what I do, and I do it daily.

I've got a million hobbies, but my favorites are retro gaming, drinking coffee, riding my bike, creative coding, illustration, and the outdoors. I play air guitar masterfully. I also love eating ramen.

I'm currently seeking opportunities in UI/UX development and design. Feel free to contact me if you need help with any of your web projects, or if you just want to grab a beer sometime. Cheers!

-Joel
