ABOUT THE PROJECT

A good friend started an NPO to assist the disaster affected region of Tohoku, Japan. The company's mission is to engage people with the area through volunteering, tourism, and outdoor recreation.

Considering Earthcamp's grass-roots style, I styled their Wordpress blog with some PHP and CSS alterations to give them a more rustic look and give visitors a better feel for the company.
